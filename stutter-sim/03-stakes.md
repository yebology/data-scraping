## Kasus Real

### Kasus 1: Olivia Farrelly vs Advance Office Products (New Zealand, 2020)
- Olivia di-hire sebagai Customer Service
- Employer dengar dia gagap di telepon → panggil meeting → **dipecat**
- Pengadilan (ERA): **diskriminasi.** Perusahaan bayar **$22.000** untuk hurt & humiliation + lost earnings
- [Sumber](https://www.stuttering.co.nz/news/unjustified-dismissal-supporting-people-who-stutter-in-the-workplace/)

### Kasus 2: Salas v. NYC Department of Investigation (US, 2018)
- Karyawan gagap di-bully dan di-mock oleh rekan kerja
- Pengadilan terima klaim **"hostile work environment"** di bawah ADA
- [Sumber](https://pospislaw.com/blog/2018/04/15/plaintiff-mocked-for-stuttering-states-hostile-work-environment-claim-under-the-americans-with-disabilities-act/)

### Indonesia: UU No. 8 Tahun 2016 tentang Penyandang Disabilitas
- Perusahaan **wajib** inklusif
- Non-compliance = risiko audit dan sanksi

## Dampak ke Perusahaan (Ringkas)

1. **Kehilangan talent** — 2.8 juta orang gagap, banyak highly qualified tapi di-reject karena bias
2. **Risiko lawsuit** — $40-75K per kasus
3. **Non-compliance** — UU Disabilitas 2016
4. **Reputational damage** — 1 kasus viral = brand trust turun
