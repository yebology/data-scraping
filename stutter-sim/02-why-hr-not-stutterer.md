# Kenapa yang Harus Belajar itu HR, Bukan Orang Gagap?

## 1. Terapi gagap TERBUKTI tidak bisa cure secara permanen

> "Natural spontaneous remission accounts for approximately 60-80% of all children recovering from stuttering. Despite our best efforts, **no protocol has ever shown its effectiveness separate from natural recovery rates.**"
— [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16195193/)

> Meta-analysis pada orang dewasa gagap: "**No significant pooled difference** between intervention and comparator groups in improving communication and psychosocial functioning."
— [PubMed 2020](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33242235/)

**Artinya:** Terapi untuk orang gagap dewasa secara statistik tidak terbukti lebih efektif dari tidak terapi. Yang "sembuh" kebanyakan anak yang memang akan recover sendiri. Melatih orang gagap = outcome uncertain.

## 2. Communication Partner Training TERBUKTI effective

> "Communication partner interventions were found to be **highly effective** across a range of participants."
— [PubMed 2015](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26059542/)

> Simulated training meningkatkan **clinical empathy, paraphrasing, dan eye gaze** secara signifikan.
— [ASHA/PubMed 2022](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36332141/)

> Online communication partner training → effective untuk healthcare professionals.
— [PMC 2024](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10952497/)

**Artinya:** Melatih ORANG LAIN (communication partner) secara evidence terbukti lebih effective daripada melatih orang gagap sendiri.

## 3. Impact 1 HR jauh lebih besar dari 1 orang gagap

- 1 recruiter handle **15-40 open req** secara bersamaan ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/recruiter-workload-reality-shally-steckerl-j7u0e))
- Per posisi, HR interview **8-20 kandidat** (industry data)
- Rata-rata recruiter 2026: **14 req** per cycle ([Pin.com](https://www.pin.com/blog/recruiter-capacity-benchmarks/))
- Hitung kasar: 1 HR × 14 req × 10 interview = **~140 interview/tahun**
- Dengan 1% prevalensi gagap → 1 HR ketemu ~1-2 orang gagap/tahun
- Seumur karir (20+ tahun): 1 HR yang trained = **20-40 orang gagap** mendapat treatment lebih fair

> **Latih 1 orang gagap = bantu 1 orang (outcome uncertain). Latih 1 HR = protect semua orang gagap yang dia interview seumur karir.**

## 4. Reaksi lingkungan terbukti MEMPERPARAH gagap

> "Adverse reactions from peers and negative self-perceptions may contribute to mental health issues in individuals with stuttering."
— [NCBI/NIH StatPearls](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK603738/)

> Listeners respond with "negative emotional arousal at behavioral, physiological, and emotional levels" — ini TRIGGER anxiety loop → gagap makin parah.
— [ECU Study](https://thescholarship.ecu.edu/items/fdc3cfcc-61c0-47be-bc59-a545632a7a39/full)

## 5. Social Model of Disability (WHO, UNICEF, UK Government)

> "Disability is something imposed ON TOP of impairments, by the way we are unnecessarily isolated and excluded from full participation in society."
— [MDPI](https://www.mdpi.com/article/10.3390/disabilities2030039)

> "The social model seeks to CHANGE SOCIETY to accommodate people with impairment. It does NOT seek to change persons with impairment to accommodate society."
— [People with Disability Australia](https://pwd.org.au/resources/models-of-disability/)

> "Psychosocial disability arises when people who stutter interact in a social environment that PRESENTS BARRIERS to their equality with others."
— [Disabled World](https://www.disabled-world.com/communication/psychosocial-disability.php)

Paper spesifik: **"Understanding Stuttering Through the Prism of Social Model of Disability"** — [ResearchGate 2021](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/355037228_Understanding_Stuttering_Through_the_Prism_of_Social_Model_of_Disability)

## Ringkasan Evidence

| Approach | Evidence | Impact per effort |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Latih orang gagap (medical model) | "No significant difference" vs tanpa terapi (dewasa) | 1 orang, outcome uncertain |
| Latih communication partner/HR (social model) | "Highly effective" — terbukti improve skill & empathy | Semua orang gagap yang HR tsb interview seumur karir |

**Kesimpulan:** Evidence support social model. Semua app di dunia ikut medical model. Kita yang pertama ikut social model.
