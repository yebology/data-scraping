# Track Fit — Health (GarudaHacks 7.0)

## Deskripsi Track

> "Health is more than just access to hospitals. It includes prevention, **mental well-being**, nutrition, environment, and **community support**. In Indonesia, disparities persist between urban and rural populations, with challenges in healthcare access, health literacy, affordability, and **cultural attitudes toward wellbeing**."

## Keselarasan

| Kata Kunci Track | Fit |
|------------------|-----|
| "Mental well-being" | Orang gagap punya anxiety/depresi akibat reaksi buruk lingkungan. Kita kurangi trigger-nya dari sumber. |
| "Community support" | Melatih HR (bagian dari "community" profesional) jadi support system, bukan barrier. |
| "Cultural attitudes toward wellbeing" | Literally mengubah attitudes/stigma terhadap orang gagap di workplace. |
| "Disparities" | 1% formal employment untuk disabilitas. 85% employer admit bias. Kita address gap ini. |
| "Prevention" | Mencegah discrimination, exclusion, dan mental health decline sebelum terjadi. |

## Kenapa Ini Health, Bukan "Just HR Tool"

Karena employment discrimination terhadap orang gagap bukan cuma masalah karir — ini masalah **kesehatan:**
- Exclusion → isolation → depresi → suicide ideation
- Unemployment → poverty → gak bisa afford healthcare
- Chronic stress dari repeated rejection → physical health deterioration
- WHO recognize employment sebagai **social determinant of health**

Solve hiring discrimination = solve health outcome untuk 2.8 juta orang.
