# Revenue Model

## Streams

| Stream | Customer | Model | Why They Pay |
|--------|----------|-------|-------------|
| B2B Corporate Training | Perusahaan besar (DEI budget) | Per-employee per-module (Rp 50-100rb/orang) | Hindari lawsuit $40-75K + comply UU Disabilitas |
| B2B Institutional | Universitas, RS | Annual license (Rp 5-20jt/tahun) | Kampus wajib inclusive per Permendikbud |
| B2G | Kemenaker, BUMN | Contract/tender | Audit inklusi, compliance |
| B2C (secondary) | Individual HR professional | Freemium / Rp 50rb/bulan | Career development |

## Cost-Benefit

> Training ini: **Rp 50.000/orang.**
> Lawsuit kalau ketahuan discriminate: **$40-75K (~Rp 640jt-1.2M).**
> ROI = **12.800x** return on investment per kasus yang dicegah.

## Target Customer Awal

Perusahaan besar yang sudah punya budget DEI training — biasanya multinasional (Unilever, Bank Mandiri, Telkom, Grab, GoTo) atau yang pernah kena masalah inklusi. Mereka sudah spend money untuk training anti-harassment, unconscious bias, dll. Ini jadi **modul tambahan** di portfolio training mereka.
