## 2. Suara ibu = biological stress buffer

> "Secure attachment has an important **buffering role in the response to stress** by the reduction of the negative experience and its **associated physiological response** (cortisol)."
— [Frontiers in Behavioral Neuroscience 2022](https://journal.frontiersin.org/article/10.3389/fnbeh.2022.954977)

> "Maternal caregiving behavior shapes cortisol reactivity to stress."
— [PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4170024/)

Anak yang dengar suara ibu → cortisol turun → calm → sleep better → develop better.
Anak yang TIDAK dengar suara ibu → cortisol tinggi kronis → brain development terganggu.

> "Your brain stores the memory of her voice and turns it into an **internal dialogue that can be comforting in moments of stress.**"
