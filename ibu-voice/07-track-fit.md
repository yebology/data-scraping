# Track Fit — Health (GarudaHacks 7.0)

## Deskripsi Track

> "Health is more than just access to hospitals. It includes prevention, **mental well-being**, nutrition, environment, and **community support**. In Indonesia, **disparities** persist between urban and rural populations, with challenges in healthcare access, health literacy, affordability, and **cultural attitudes toward wellbeing**."

## Keselarasan

| Kata Kunci Track | Fit |
|------------------|-----|
| "Mental well-being" | Anak TKI alami depresi, anxiety, loneliness akibat absence ibu. Kita address mental well-being anak secara langsung. |
| "Prevention" | Mencegah kerusakan neurologis permanen di usia kritis SEBELUM terlambat. Preventive intervention. |
| "Community support" | Teknologi sebagai bridge yang support keluarga TKI yang terpisah jarak. |
| "Disparities" | Anak TKI (rural, ekonomi bawah) vs anak yang orang tuanya ada = disparity kesehatan mental yang masif. |
| "Cultural attitudes toward wellbeing" | Di Indonesia, TKI sering dipandang "sacrifice for family" — tapi dampak ke anak TIDAK dibicarakan. Kita bring awareness. |

## Kenapa Ini Health, Bukan Sekadar "Family App"

1. **Cortisol regulation** = health outcome (stress hormone → physical health impact)
2. **Brain development** = health outcome (neurological development di usia kritis)
3. **Mental health** = health outcome (depression, anxiety, behavioral problems)
4. **Sleep quality** = health outcome (bedtime routine → better sleep → better overall health)
5. WHO recognize **attachment** dan **early childhood development** sebagai fundamental health determinants

Ini bukan "app buat kangen-kangenan." Ini **health intervention** yang targeted ke populasi vulnerable (anak TKI) menggunakan neuroscience-backed approach.
