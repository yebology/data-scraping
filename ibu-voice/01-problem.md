# Problem + Kenapa Urgent

## Inti Masalah

Jutaan ibu Indonesia kerja sebagai TKI di luar negeri. Anak-anak mereka (usia kritis 0-11 tahun) tumbuh tanpa mendengar suara ibu setiap hari — karena timezone beda, biaya telepon, anak belum bisa pegang HP sendiri, atau ibu kerja 14-16 jam/hari dan gak sempat nelpon.

Ini bukan soal "kangen." Ini kerusakan biologis yang terjadi sekarang.

## Kenapa Urgent

### 1. Otak anak butuh suara ibu untuk develop normal

fMRI study buktikan otak anak process suara ibu di area reward + emotion — ini yang bangun attachment aman. Tanpa exposure rutin ke suara ibu → attachment gak terbentuk → stress system anak permanently altered.

> "Young children's brain responds differently and more strongly to mother's voice vs other voices — activating reward system, emotion processing, and face processing regions."
— [PMC/NIH fMRI study](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6334756/)

### 2. Kerusakannya permanen kalau terlambat

> "Over half a century of converging clinical and animal research indicates that early life experiences induce **enduring neuroplasticity** of the HPA-axis and the developing brain."
— [Frontiers in Endocrinology](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fendo.2014.00033/full)

Window usia 0-7 tahun itu terbatas. Setiap malam tanpa suara ibu = opportunity yang hilang selamanya. Efeknya gak bisa di-undo di masa dewasa.

### 3. Data Indonesia confirm kerusakan sudah terjadi

> "Left-behind children tend to experience **more emotional and behavioral problems** than non-LBC."
— [PMC Indonesia study](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6532385/)

> "The absence of mothers significantly increases vulnerability to **depression, anxiety, and difficulties in forming emotional bonds.**"
— [Frontiers in Psychology 2024](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2024.1407733/full)

> "Parental migration negatively impacted social well-being — reflected in **negative labelling from friends** and changes in familial roles."
— [MDPI 2024](https://www.mdpi.com/1660-4601/21/6/793/xml)

### 4. Scale masif

- Mayoritas TKI Indonesia = perempuan (ibu) — [PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6532385/)
- Ini bukan ratusan anak — ini jutaan anak dalam fase development paling kritis
- "Most migrant workers are females with children, creating a **huge number** of left-behind children"

### 5. Komunikasi yang jarang = predictor loneliness

> "Left-behind children would be **more susceptible to loneliness** if they... **rarely communicated with their parents.**"
— [Emerald, Indonesia study](https://www.emerald.com/insight/content/doi/10.1108/JPMH-04-2018-0023/full/html)

> "Good parent-child communication is related with **better psychological resilience.**"
— [MDPI](https://mdpi.com/1660-4601/18/10/5123)

## Ringkas

Setiap malam yang lewat tanpa suara ibu = satu lagi malam dimana otak anak kehilangan kesempatan membangun attachment yang sehat. Window-nya terbatas. Ini terjadi sekarang, ke jutaan anak, dan gak ada solusi yang address ini.
